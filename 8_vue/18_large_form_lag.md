# Form lớn bị lag

- `v-model.lazy`
- Debounce validate
- Thư viện: VeeValidate

## Chi tiết câu trả lời

Optimize large forms.

### `v-model.lazy`

- Update on change/blur, not input.
- Reduce updates.

### Debounce validate

- Debounce validation calls.
- Wait user stop typing.

### Thư viện: VeeValidate

- Powerful validation library.
- Built-in rules, async support.

VeeValidate recommended cho complex forms.
