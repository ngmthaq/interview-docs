# Directive là gì?

- `v-if`, `v-for`, `v-show`
- Custom directive dùng khi nào?

## Chi tiết câu trả lời

Directive trong Vue.

### Built-in directives

- `v-if`: Conditional rendering.
- `v-for`: List rendering.
- `v-show`: Toggle visibility.

### Custom directive

- Extend HTML behavior.
- Dùng khi logic DOM low-level.
- Ví dụ: focus, tooltip.

Directive manipulate DOM directly.
